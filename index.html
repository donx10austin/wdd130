Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located in the western part of Africa. with a population of over 250 million people, the country is well known for its hospitality and its citizens are known for peace, accomodating, hardwork and God fearing. There're several tribes in Nigeria majorly Igbo, Hausa and the Yoruba. The country is blessed with many mineral resources and they're one of the major exporters of Crude, Gold, its citizens are mostly farmers, doctors, bankers and enginers. There're 36 states in Nigeria and Lagos is one of them with the highest sea port in Africa and Abuja as its capital.
