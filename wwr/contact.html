Our History

Our Founding

Our journey began in 1995 with a shared love for the river and a vision to offer unforgettable rafting experiences. A group of passionate adventurers came together, laying the foundation for what White Water Rafting is today. From our humble beginnings with just a few rafts, we have grown into a leading provider of white water adventures in the region, always staying true to our core values of safety, adventure, and respect for nature.

Milestones

Over the years, White Water Rafting has reached significant milestones. We expanded our range of trips to cater to different skill levels and interests, from family-friendly floats to challenging expeditions. We invested in top-of-the-line equipment and rigorous training for our guides to ensure the highest safety standards. Our commitment to sustainable tourism has also led us to partner with local conservation efforts, preserving the natural beauty of the rivers we navigate for future generations to enjoy.
